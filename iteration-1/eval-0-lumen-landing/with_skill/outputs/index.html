Built for teams that
actually work.

Every feature is designed around how distributed teams communicate — not how office teams used to.

01

Timezone-aware reminders

Nudges go out at the right local time for each team member. No 3 AM pings, no missed updates from someone in another continent.

02

Structured, not freeform

Guided prompts keep updates consistent. What did you do, what's next, what's blocking you — every time, from everyone.

03

Live work context

Connects to GitHub, Jira, Linear. Your commits and issues surface automatically so you spend less time writing and more time doing.

04

Blocker triage

Blockers get flagged and routed to the right people instantly. Managers see what needs attention without reading every update.

05

Digest digests

One clean summary per day, per team. Read it in 90 seconds, know exactly where everything stands.

06

Threads, not noise

Reply directly to an update. Conversations stay attached to context — not buried in a Slack channel from three days ago.
